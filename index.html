EXCHANGE

How to initiate Exchange:

  • Login to your Account. You'll find an exchange button next to your respective order.
  • Click on the button to initiate an exchange request. (Please note:- The exchange button will be visible 24 hours after the order has been delivered.)

Note: The exchange request can be raised only once for one order. Hence please try and check the products carefully you have received in one order before
initiating the exchange as we do not provide multiple exchanges for the same order.
